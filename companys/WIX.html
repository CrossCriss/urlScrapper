Про компанію

Wix.com is a leading cloud-based development platform with millions of users worldwide. We make it easy for everyone to create a beautiful, professional web presence.

Our work environment is one of the most unique places on the planet. Wix offices in Tel Aviv, New York, San Francisco, Vilnius, Kyiv and Dnipro are home to a colorful group of professionals (and their dogs) who bring passion, skill and innovation to ensure we provide a product that people love to use.

Be inspired. Be heard. Be innovative. Be a part of something big.
